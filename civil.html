About the Department

The Department of Civil Engineering at SRKR Engineering College, Bhimavaram, was started in 1980 and is as old as the department. It has earned a reputation in Andhra Pradesh and the coastal districts for its dedicated teaching and consultancy. The department pulls out all the stops to create outstanding engineers with advanced teaching techniques and learning aids for undergraduate students and a selected section of post-graduate students (Structural Engineering). Students made experts in technical aspects and interpersonal skills, a vital ingredient to excel in the fast-paced world.
