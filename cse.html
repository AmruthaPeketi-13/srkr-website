About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at SRKR Engineering College is dedicated to academic excellence and innovation in technology. Established in 2001, the department has grown into a vibrant hub of research and development, offering B.Tech, M.Tech, and research programs.

The curriculum is designed to provide strong theoretical and practical foundations in software, hardware, and computational theory. The department has state-of-the-art labs, dedicated faculty, and active industry collaboration.

Under Graduate Programmes offered by CSE Department B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering - 4 Years, B.Tech Computer Science and Design - 4 Years (Since 2021), B.Tech Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning(Since 2021), B.Tech(CSE) - IoT & Cyber Security including Block Chain Technology (Since 2022)
